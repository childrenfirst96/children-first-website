Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Children First Foundation Inc exists to provide vulnerable children in Ivory Coast with the support, protection, and opportunities they need to thrive. Through meals, school support, hygiene assistance, and community outreach, we help children build healthier and brighter futures.

🌱

Hope

We believe every child has the right to a hopeful, opportunity-rich future.

🤝

Dignity

We serve with deep respect, honoring the worth and potential of every child.

🚀

Opportunity

We open doors through nutrition, education, and community support.

What We Do

What We Provide

Our programs address the most urgent needs of vulnerable children across Ivory Coast with compassion and impact.

🍽

Nutrition & Meal Support

We provide nutritious meals to children facing hunger and food insecurity, ensuring no child goes to sleep hungry.

📚

Educational Materials & School Assistance

We supply school materials and resources to help children stay in school and achieve academic success.

🧴

Hygiene & Basic Wellness Support

We distribute hygiene kits and promote healthy habits, protecting children's well-being and dignity.

🏘

Community-Centered Outreach

We work closely with local leaders and families to create lasting, sustainable impact in communities.

Support Children in Ivory Coast

At Children First Foundation, our mission is to provide children in Ivory Coast with access to nutritious food, education, and essential daily necessities. We are committed to creating opportunities that help children build a brighter and more secure future.

As we begin this journey, we are seeking the support of compassionate individuals and communities to help launch our programs and expand our impact. Every contribution—large or small—can make a meaningful difference in the life of a child.
