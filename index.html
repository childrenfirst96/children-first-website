About Children First

Children First was founded on a simple belief: every child in Ivory Coast deserves a chance to dream, grow, and shine. Yet thousands of children face daily hardships — hunger, illness, unsafe living conditions, or the lack of a school to call their own.


At Children First, we are dedicated to restoring hope and opening doors for these children through nutritious meals, healthcare, quality education, and community support.

Our Work In Action

My Story

I know firsthand how difficult life can be for children growing up in poverty. My father didn't have a stable job, and we often struggled to have enough to eat.


After my father passed away, continuing my education became nearly impossible — a reality faced by thousands of children in Ivory Coast.


I founded Children First because I want to give these children the support I wish I had. Every child deserves the chance to thrive, to learn, and to dream.

— Banney Dro, Founder

Support Children in Ivory Coast
